CalmNest for Organizations

Private, AI-guided mental health support that lives where your teams work — Slack, Microsoft Teams, and email — with clinicians in the loop when it matters.

How it works

  • Fast access: Employees open CalmNest in Slack/Teams, describe what’s going on, and get actionable guidance in minutes.
  • Personalized playbooks: Brief, practical steps for stress, sleep, conflict, focus, and burnout.
  • Human escalation: With consent, cases can route to your EAP, in-house wellbeing team, or CalmNest clinicians.

Why orgs use us

  • Confidential by design: Individual chats stay private. Orgs only see anonymized utilization trends.
  • Zero heavy lift: Deploy in a day, no new logins. SSO supported.
  • Measurable outcomes: NPS and quick check-ins to track impact over time.

Security & compliance

  • Data controls: Region pinning, retention windows, and audit logs.
  • Standards: Encryption in transit/at rest, least-privilege access, DPA available.
  • Privacy: No employer access to individual conversations.

Rollout options

  • Pilot with one team (50–200 people)
  • Company-wide with custom playbooks and escalation rules
  • Quarterly insights: anonymized trends and recommendations
